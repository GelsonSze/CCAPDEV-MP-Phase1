Genre: Action Adventure








Hollow Knight is a 2017 Metroidvania action-adventure game developed and published by Team Cherry. In the game, players control the Knight, a nameless warrior unaware of their own origins. The Knight explores Hallownest, a once thriving kingdom whose inhabitants deprived of their minds. The game itself is set in diverse locations, and it features friendly and hostile bug-like characters and numerous bosses. Players have the opportunity to unlock new abilities as they explore each location, along with pieces of lore and flavor text that are spread throughout the world (Wikipedia,n.d.).
